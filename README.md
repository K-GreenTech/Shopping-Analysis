# shopping-behavior

## Analytical Questions

What are 6 analytical questions you can ask about this dataset?

1. What is the ratio of male to female who purchased items?
2. Which age group had the most overall orders?
3. Which season experienced more purchases per state?
4. Which item of clothing had the most purchases and in which season?
5. Which method of payment is most used overall?
6. Did those who made no previous purchases or have a low previous purchase history use promotional codes?
